Smoked Chuck Steak Recipe

Ingredients

  • Crosshairs Provider Spice Seasoning (To Taste)
  • Fowl Provider Spice Seasoning (To Taste)
  • Yellow Mustard as a rub binder

Directions

  1. Preheat smoker to 275oF.
  2. Rub your meat, covering all sides with a thin coat of mustard.
  3. Liberally cover the meat with "Fowl" and "Crosshairs" Provider Spice Seasoning.
  4. Cook on the smoker until the internal temperature reaches 120-125oF.
  5. Remove the meat from the smoker and let it rest for 20
  6. Preheat an oven or your smoker to 475-500oF.
  7. Cook the meat until desired doneness. Best at around 130Â°F internal temperature.
  8. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
  9. Slice and enjoy!
