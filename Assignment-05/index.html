Snapshot

Ten Rules for Finding a Good Hunting Partner is a universal concept in many different facets of life. These same ten principles can be applied to finding a spouse, business partner, or even just a good friend. The stories in this book are from true events and was inspired by a pet project to introduce a brand-new person into the backpack hunting world. Through the process of teaching this individual how to hunt, great friendships and partnerships were born.
